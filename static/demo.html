Keyboard Controls:

W / S - Move left paddle up/down

SPACE - Pause/Resume game

R - Reset game

M - Toggle sound effects

Audio Experience

Atmospheric Sound Design

The game features a unique audio experience:

  • Continuous low-frequency hum (60Hz) for ambient atmosphere
  • Periodic deep booms (45Hz) every 8-15 seconds
  • Low-pass filtering for muffled, distant effect
  • Very quiet volume (0.02-0.03) so it doesn't interfere with gameplay

Press M to toggle sound on/off during gameplay.
